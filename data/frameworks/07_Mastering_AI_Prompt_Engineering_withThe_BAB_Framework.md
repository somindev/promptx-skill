# 

Mastering AI Prompt Engineering with

The BAB Framework

Originally designed as a copywriting formula, the BAB Framework (Before, After, Bridge) has been successfully adapted for AI prompt engineering. Like PAS (Pain, Agitate, Solution) and AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action), BAB was created to structure persuasive text but has found new life in guiding AI models to deliver precise and impactful responses. Its emphasis on storytelling and emotional resonance makes it an ideal tool for crafting prompts that connect user needs with actionable solutions.

Each step in the BAB Framework plays a distinct role: **Before** paints the user's problem or challenge vividly, **After** envisions the ideal solution or state, and **Bridge** connects the problem and solution, highlighting how AI or a specific product/service provides the desired outcome. This structure ensures that prompts are not only clear but also compelling, helping users achieve meaningful results from AI interactions.

## 

Transform Your Copywriting and Prompting with

The BAB Framework

The BAB Framework (Before, After, Bridge) is a simple yet powerful tool for structuring persuasive prompts and copy. It guides AI interactions by focusing on three core elements:

  * **Before:** Highlight the current problem, challenge, or pain point faced by the user or audience.
  * **After:** Paint a vivid picture of the ideal outcome or solution, showing the transformation they desire.
  * **Bridge:** Connect the problem and solution by introducing your product, service, or idea as the key to achieving that outcome.



With this straightforward structure, BAB excels at creating emotionally compelling narratives, making it ideal for marketing, customer engagement, and persuasive communication.

## 

BAB vs AIDA Framework: Choosing the Right Approach

Both frameworks are powerful tools for marketing and prompting, but they cater to different objectives. Here's a comparison:

Aspect| BAB| AIDA  
---|---|---  
**Focus**|  Emotion-driven storytelling| Attention, interest, desire, and action  
**Best for**|  Persuasion through problem-solution narratives| Structured, step-by-step conversion processes  
**Flexibility**|  Excellent for emotionally charged copy| Great for capturing and converting leads  
  
Choose BAB for emotional resonance and storytelling, or explore [AIDA here](/knowledge-base/prompt-frameworks/the-aida-framework/) for conversion-focused strategies.

## 

BAB Framework Overview

  * **Before:** Describe the user's problem or challenge in a relatable and engaging way.
  * **After:** Present an ideal future state or solution that addresses the problem.
  * **Bridge:** Highlight how a specific product, service, or strategy connects the problem to the solution.

## 

BAB Framework vs. PAS and AIDA Frameworks

### Strengths

  * **Storytelling:** Builds a narrative that engages users emotionally and motivates action.
  * **Simplicity:** Easy to understand and apply across different tasks.
  * **Versatility:** Suitable for marketing, support, and creative problem-solving.



### Weaknesses

  * **Limited Detail:** May require additional frameworks for complex or technical tasks.
  * **Dependent on Context:** Effectiveness relies on how well the problem and solution are framed.



## 

Applications

Where to Use the BAB Framework

The BAB Framework is ideal for tasks requiring clarity, empathy, and motivation. Use it for:

  * Cold email campaigns
  * Marketing and branding strategies
  * Customer support solutions
  * Educational content creation
  * Persuasive presentations



## 

Conclusion

By adapting its storytelling roots to AI prompt engineering, the BAB Framework offers a simple yet powerful way to create effective prompts. Whether you’re crafting marketing campaigns, designing user-friendly tools, or solving customer problems, BAB ensures that your prompts are both relatable and actionable. Its versatility and emotional appeal make it a standout choice for building trust and delivering results in AI-driven tasks.
