## 

FOCUS Framework Overview

  * **Function:** Clearly define the function or purpose of the prompt, outlining what you need the AI to do
    * **Objective:** Specify the objective or goal that the response should achieve, providing a clear target for the AI's output
      * **Context:** Offer context to enrich the AI's understanding of the prompt, including any relevant background information or specific conditions
        * **Utility:** Highlight the utility or usefulness of the expected response, explaining how the information or output will be used
          * **Specifications:** Detail any specifications or requirements for the response, such as format, length, or key points that need to be addressed

 __




### Prompt example using the FOCUS Framework

Let's consider we're seeking AI assistance for generating a report on emerging technology trends in renewable energy. Here's how a FOCUS-structured prompt might be crafted:

You are tasked with generating a report. The report should identify key emerging trends in renewable energy technology and provide an analysis of their potential impact. Given the global emphasis on reducing carbon emissions and the increasing investment in sustainable energy, the report should focus on technologies with the potential for significant environmental and economic benefits. The report will be used by our strategic planning team to inform investment and development decisions in the renewable energy sector. The report should be structured to include an executive summary, detailed analysis of each identified trend, examples of current applications, and potential future implications, formatted as a PDF document of 10-15 pages.

## 

Strengths and weaknesses of the FOCUS Framework

### Strengths

  * **Goal-Oriented Prompts:** By emphasizing the function and objective, it ensures that prompts are directly aligned with the desired outcome
  * **Enhanced Contextual Understanding:** Providing context helps the AI to generate more relevant and nuanced responses
  * **Clear Expectations:** Specifications about the response format or content guide the AI to produce outputs that meet user needs more precisely



### Weaknesses

  * **Requires Detailed Input:** Crafting an effective FOCUS prompt may demand a significant upfront investment in thought and planning
  * **Risk of Over-Specification:** There's a potential to constrain the AI's creative or exploratory capabilities by detailing too many specifications
